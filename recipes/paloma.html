Paloma Recipe

Description

It turns out the Paloma, not the Margarita, is Mexico's most beloved cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz Grapefruit Spindrift
  • 2 oz Rum
  • 1 oz Simple Syrup
  • 1/2 Lime

Steps

  1. Squeeze lime into glass over ice.
  2. In a cocktail mixer, shake together remaining ingredients.
  3. Pour into glass and enjoy.
