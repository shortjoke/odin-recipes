Pina Colada Recipe

Description

The pi√±a colada is a cocktail made with rum, cream of coconut or coconut milk, and pineapple juice, usually served either blended or shaken with ice. It may be garnished with either a pineapple wedge, maraschino cherry, or both. There are two versions of the drink, both originating in Puerto Rico.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Coco Lopez
  • 1 can of Coconut Milk
  • 8 oz Frozen Pineapple
  • 8 oz Rum

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Turn the blender on until all ingredients are thoroughly blended.
  3. Pour into tall glass and enjoy.
